I’m Zhentao Huang, a PhD student in computer science at the University of Guelph, advised by Professor Minglun Gong. My research is in 3D reconstruction and neural rendering — how to represent, render, and edit 3D scenes efficiently — spanning Gaussian Splatting (Textured-GS), editable neural radiance fields (SealD-NeRF), and multi-view stereo.

Feel free to reach out through any of the links below.
