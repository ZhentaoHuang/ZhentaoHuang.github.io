I’m Zhentao Huang, a graduate researcher in computer science at the University of Guelph, working on 3D reconstruction and neural rendering. My research looks at how to represent, render, and edit 3D scenes efficiently — including Gaussian Splatting (Textured-GS) and editable neural radiance fields (SealD-NeRF), as well as applications in medical image analysis.
